The Attack Cycle

Execution blueprints for psychological and systemic exploitation.

2.1 Stage 1: Reconnaissance and Profiling (The Triangulation Protocol)

The initial stage creates a predictive behavioral model. It involves an OSINT Scan (mapping public digital footprint) and Access Agent Infiltration. This culminates in the creation of a detailed Vulnerability Dossier.

2.1.1 Vulnerability Dossier (Tactical Utility)

  • Trauma: Documented for its ability to create psychological weak points, triggering an unconscious, predictable, and disproportionate reaction.
  • Fear: Mapped to create a menu of high-leverage threats, serving as leverage points to manipulate behavior or coerce silence.
  • Emotional Sensitivities: Identifying specific Emotional Triggers and Core Insecurities for exploitation through relentless micro-aggressions.

2.2 Stage 2: The Exploitation Cascade (Multi-Domain Attrition)

The execution phase, designed to overwhelm defenses and establish total narrative control.

2.2.1 Reputation Assassination (The Smear)

  • Reviving Past Offenses: Old incidents are resurrected, disproportionately exaggerated, and disseminated to establish a spurious "pattern" of negative behavior.
  • Twisting Innocent Interactions: Benign actions (e.g., an act of kindness) are deliberately reinterpreted with malicious intent (e.g., "stalking").
  • Social Exile Wrapped in Fake Compassion: Profound social isolation is achieved through a deceptive display of "concern" while simultaneously spreading rumors leading to ostracization.

3.1 Core Tactics of Psychological Weaponization

Setup by Reaction / Engineered Outrage
Deliberate provocation until the target snaps. The resulting justifiable outburst is twisted and used as "fabricated evidence" to support a predetermined negative narrative.
Gaslighting and Fabricated Reality
Systematic, sustained psychological manipulation designed to make the target question their own memory and sanity. This erosion of self-trust is amplified by Memory Discrepancy Amplification.
DARVO (Deny, Attack, Reverse Victim and Offender)
The deflection strategy where the orchestrator will first Deny the abuse, then Attack the target for raising it, and then Reverse the roles of Victim and Offender.
Weaponized Authenticity
The target's commitment to unfiltered truth (e.g., disclosures of personal vulnerabilities) is systematically collected, meticulously reframed to fit a negative narrative, and deployed as evidence of instability or malevolent intent.

This tactic co-opts legitimate institutions and public platforms, leveraging authority and trust to validate false narratives. This includes Systemic Co-option (e.g., Law Enforcement Co-option) and Weaponized Compliance (using internal investigations to discredit victims).

5.1 ANCODI: Emotional Progression Toward Violence

ANCODI (Anger, Contempt, Disgust) represents a volatile emotional progression that fuels intergroup aggression. This framework explains how campaigns escalate from isolated harassment to coordinated violence.

The Three-Stage Emotional Cascade

Stage Emotional State Function Manifestation
Stage 1 Anger Initial mobilization to remove obstacles Target framed as "problem to be solved," triggering protective/defensive response in group.
Stage 2 Contempt Moral superiority and devaluation of outgroup Target viewed as inferior, unworthy of respect or fair treatment. Justifies exclusion and cruelty.
Stage 3 Disgust Most dangerous stage—frames outgroup as contamination Target dehumanized as threat to group purity. Justifies "elimination" as necessary cleansing.

5.2 The Moral Panic Engine

Moral Panic provides the initial outrage that seeds the ANCODI sequence. A target is constructed as a "Folk Devil"—a public persona embodying societal fears or prejudices, painting them as existential threat to group values.

Folk Devil Construction Process

  • Identification: Target selected based on existing vulnerabilities or marginal status
  • Exaggeration: Minor behaviors amplified into major threats through selective reporting and rumor
  • Symbolization: Target becomes symbol of broader societal fear (predator, criminal, outsider threat)
  • Mobilization: Community "concern" transforms into collective action against the Folk Devil

This construction generates the Anger phase (collective mobilization), which naturally escalates to Contempt (moral superiority) and eventually Disgust (dehumanization).

5.3 DARVO Integration: Reversing Victim and Offender

Once ANCODI progression reaches the Disgust phase, DARVO (Deny, Attack, Reverse Victim and Offender) provides justification framework:

  1. Deny: Campaign actors deny coordinated harassment ("We're just concerned citizens")
  2. Attack: Target's defensive responses are framed as aggressive behavior proving the Folk Devil narrative
  3. Reverse: Orchestrators position themselves as victims protecting community from dangerous outsider

DARVO allows perpetrators to feel morally justified while executing violence—they are "protecting" the community from contamination (Disgust frame) rather than attacking an innocent person.

5.4 Digital-to-Physical Pipeline

ANCODI-charged online discourse frequently transitions into real-world harm through predictable escalation patterns:

Escalation Pathway

  1. Online Narrative Building: Folk Devil construction occurs in digital spaces (social media, forums, group chats)
  2. Coordinated Amplification: Multiple actors share and reinforce narrative, moving through Anger → Contempt → Disgust phases
  3. Intelligence Gathering: Doxxing reveals target's physical location, routines, vulnerabilities
  4. Distributed Action: Multiple actors execute harassment in coordinated but deniable ways (anonymous tips, property damage, intimidation)
  5. Physical Confrontation: Terminal escalation—direct violence justified by fully established Disgust frame

Case Pattern Examples

  • Pizzagate: Online child-sex narrative (Disgust frame) led to armed investigation of pizza restaurant
  • Swatting Campaigns: False emergency reports weaponize law enforcement against targets
  • Vigilante Actions: Online militia coordination produces real-world "patrols" and confrontations

5.5 Recognition Markers: Identifying ANCODI Progression

Targets can recognize ANCODI escalation through specific warning signs:

Anger Phase Indicators
Increased frequency of "concerned" contacts, anonymous tips to authorities, coordinated rumors positioning you as "problem." Rhetoric focuses on "need to do something about [target]."
Contempt Phase Indicators
Mockery and ridicule replace serious concern. You're openly discussed as inferior, crazy, untrustworthy. Social exclusion intensifies—people stop hiding their disdain.
Disgust Phase Indicators (CRITICAL)
Language shifts to contamination metaphors—you're described as "poison," "disease," "predator," or "threat to children/community." This is terminal phase before violence. Immediate extraction protocols required.

⚠️ CRITICAL THREAT INDICATOR

If you observe Disgust-phase language (contamination metaphors, dehumanization, explicit calls to "remove" or "cleanse"), the situation has reached critical danger level. This is not hyperbole—the Disgust frame psychologically authorizes violence. Activate Dead Man's Switch, document everything, consider immediate physical relocation. Do not wait to see if situation de-escalates. Disgust phase rarely reverses without external intervention or target removal.

5.6 Counter-Strategy: Disrupting ANCODI Progression

Defense against ANCODI requires interrupting the emotional progression before reaching Disgust phase:

  • Early Intervention (Anger Phase): Document coordination patterns, expose anonymous actors, create counter-narrative before Folk Devil construction solidifies
  • Narrative Disruption (Contempt Phase): Strategic Silence starves mockery of reactions, Content-as-Record provides verifiable counter-evidence, third-party validators undermine group consensus
  • Emergency Protocols (Disgust Phase): Physical safety prioritized over reputation defense, Dead Man's Switch activated, law enforcement notified with documented threats, immediate extraction from hostile environment
